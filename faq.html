What is Eve's estimated release date?

Eve is still in mid-development and away from release. Please, check our announcements (in-game) for more information. We will announce the release date as soon as we can.


The What game engine are you using to develop Eve?

Eve was made using Unity, a potent and professional game engine widely compatible with most platforms.


What software do you use for pixel art animations?

Pyxel Edit: it's an excellent software with all the simple functions needed to draw pixel art, set up sprite sheets, and preview animations. It also allows for layer management and tile placement.


What software do you use for environment art?

Photoshop is used for mockups, then Pyxel Edit to set up tilesets.


How do I report a bug?

You can report bugs in by sending an email to support@rpg-eve.com.


How can I support Eve's development??

You can support Eve's development by donating to our Ko-fi pages and sharing the game with your friends. You may also help us by finding bugs, reporting them to us, or suggesting new features.


Why can I not access the website?

The website may be blocked elsewhere without our knowledge or be down for maintenance. Please, try again later.
